    How It Works

    Adding tasks

    Type a description, optionally add a course, due date, and priority, then click Add Task or press Enter.

    Completing tasks

    Click the circle on any task to toggle it between pending and completed states.

    Deleting tasks

    Click the X button to permanently remove a task. Use Clear Completed to remove all done tasks at once.

    Auto-save

    Tasks are saved to your browser's localStorage and persist when you return to this page.
